# Right Now - What are you doing?

**App Name:** Right Now
**Subtitle:** Instant Status & Chat based on Activity

## Short Description
Share what you're doing right now and chat with others doing the same thing. No profiles, just activities.

## Long Description
**Right Now** connects you with people who are doing exactly what you are doingâ€”right now. 

Whether you're **Studyng**, **Eating**, **Working**, or just **Chilling**, find a room full of people sharing that specific moment. Join the conversation, share your vibe, and connect instantly without the pressure of permanent social profiles.

**Key Features:**

*   **Activity-Based Connection:** Choose from activities like Working, Waiting, Studying, Eating, and more.
*   **Instant Chat Rooms:** Jump straight into a chat room dedicated to your current activity.
*   **Dummy Identities:** We believe in the moment, not the history. Sign up with a fun, randomized alias (like "Happy Panda" or "Swift Fox") to keep things light and privacy-focused.
*   **Real-Time Messaging:** Fast, reliable messaging powered by Firebase.
*   **Anonymous Guest Mode:** Just want to take a peek? Jump in as a guest with a single tap.
*   **Modern Design:** Sleek, edge-to-edge dark mode interface for a comfortable experience day or night.
*   **Secure Authentication:** Sign up securely with email/password to save your alias, or go fully anonymous.

**Why Right Now?**
Social media is often about past highlights. **Right Now** is about the present moment. It's for the student looking for a study buddy at 2 AM, the remote worker wanting a quick water-cooler chat, or anyone killing time while waiting for a bus.

Download **Right Now** and share your moment!

## Technical Details

*   **Architecture:** Flutter (Dart)
*   **Backend:** Firebase (Auth, Firestore)
*   **State Management:** Provider
*   **UI/UX:** Material 3, Edge-to-Edge Design

## Privacy Policy (Summary)
We prioritize your privacy. We do not track your precise location or sell your data. Your "Dummy Identity" ensures you can chat freely without revealing personal information unless you choose to.
